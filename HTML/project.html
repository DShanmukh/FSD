Environmental impact assessment

Evaluate the impact of infrastructure projects, such as highways or dams, on local vegetation. Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) is a systematic process that evaluates the potential environmental effects of a proposed project, plan, or program before it is carried out. The primary goal of EIA is to ensure that development activities are undertaken with full consideration of their potential impacts on the environment. This process helps in making informed decisions, minimizing adverse effects, and promoting sustainable development.
